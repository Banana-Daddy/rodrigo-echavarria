Bio

The player.
The coach.

Born in Colombia. Right-handed. Clay specialist by heritage — the dirt was the surface he learned the game on, and the surface he still teaches it best on.

Turned pro in 2018 and spent the next four seasons on the ATP and ITF circuits. Career singles record of 49–44 across hard, clay, and indoor surfaces. Career high in the ATP rankings was 2020. Held the #1 ranking in Colombia. Worked with the country’s national champions in the years before stepping off tour.

Now eight years into a coaching career at Garden State Tennis Center — one of the most decorated teaching programs in the Northeast, with more than fifty ETA #1 juniors, three NCAA national champions, and players who have competed at the US Open, Wimbledon, and Roland Garros.

He works in English and Spanish. He prefers the clay.
