About the Subject

Eight years, two countries,
one game.

Born in Colombia in 1996. Right-handed clay-court specialist. Turned pro in 2018 and spent the next four seasons on the ATP and ITF circuits, posting a 49–44 career singles record and holding the #1 ranking in his home country.

Since 2018 he’s been coaching at Garden State Tennis Center in Edison, New Jersey — a program with more than fifty ETA #1 juniors, three NCAA national champions, and a teaching staff that has produced players who have competed at every Grand Slam. He’s now the club’s Director of Tournament Training.

Lessons run $110 an hour. He works in English and Spanish.
